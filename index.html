Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Atomik beta

What is Atomik Beta?
Atomik Beta is early access to our real-time solar system exploration platform. You'll get to experience cutting-edge NASA-powered simulations before the official launch, completely free.
Is the beta really free?
Yes! Beta access is completely free. We're looking for feedback from space enthusiasts, educators, and astronomy lovers to help us improve the platform before our official launch.
What simulations are included?
Beta users get access to Earth's Orbit simulation, Full Solar System view, and Space Weather Monitor. All simulations use real NASA JPL orbital data and update in real-time.
What devices are supported?
Atomik works on all modern desktop and mobile browsers. For the best experience, we recommend using Chrome, Firefox, or Safari on a device with WebGL support.
How long will the beta last?
The beta period will run for approximately 3-6 months as we gather feedback and refine the experience. Beta users will receive priority access to all future features.
Can I provide feedback?
Absolutely! We encourage all beta users to share their thoughts, report bugs, and suggest features. Use the feedback form in your account or email us directly.
